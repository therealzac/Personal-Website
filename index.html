Who I am

I am a creator. I have an edacious hunger for knowledge - the pursuit of it is holy. In short, I'm a geek. I'm the type of person that considers Mathematical Theorems a high form of art, and software development is the most exciting application of formal logic that the human race has come up with.

So, I spend my days building microcosms out of the poetry of computer code. I believe the world will change more in the 21st Century than it ever has in human history, and I'm thrilled to be a part of it.

Projects

Crashmate.io

When I first moved to SF I had a roommate from England, who was shocked to find that there is no US equivalent to spareroom.co.uk - a roommate finding service that's popular among our friends across the pond. Enter crashmate.io. Find roommates, form groups, and get into a new place quickly with people you'll love.

Elevator Wars

I've always been interested in games that can teach people things. Elevator Wars is my first venture in this direction. Learn Javascript, and have some fun doin it.

Quizzler

During App Academy, a few friends and I were commissioned to build Quizzler: an extension to App Academy's Progress Tracker that allows students to take nightly quizzes and instructors to create them.
